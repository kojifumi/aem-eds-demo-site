Absolutely. Nexara AI is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. We support deployment in your own private cloud or on-premises. Your data is never used to train models for other customers.

No. Nexara AI is designed for business teams, not just data scientists. Our no-code interface lets anyone build, train, and deploy AI models. We also offer fully managed AI services if you need additional support.
