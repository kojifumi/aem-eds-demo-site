Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How quickly can we get started with Nexara AI?

Most teams are fully onboarded within 2 weeks. Our implementation specialists guide you through data connection, model setup, and team training. No infrastructure changes required.

Is our data secure and private?

Absolutely. Nexara AI is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. We support deployment in your own private cloud or on-premises. Your data is never used to train models for other customers.

Do I need a data science team to use Nexara AI?

No. Nexara AI is designed for business teams, not just data scientists. Our no-code interface lets anyone build, train, and deploy AI models. We also offer fully managed AI services if you need additional support.

What integrations are available?

Nexara AI integrates natively with Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Snowflake, Databricks, and 200+ other platforms via REST API and pre-built connectors.

What pricing plans are available?

We offer Starter ($0/month for up to 3 users), Growth ($299/month), and Enterprise (custom pricing) plans. All plans include a 14-day free trial with full feature access — no credit card required.
